About

Cyberia is an indepedent arts organization and electronic music event series.

The organization operates at the intersection of sound, ambience, and visual systems.

Each event is constructed as a full sensory environment.

We collaborate with artists, designers, and technologists.

Our focus is on precision, atmosphere, and cultural impact.
